# Lil Big Run Club

Melbourne-based community run club for the everyday person — inclusive, welcoming, and unpretentious with a premium feel.

## Summary

Lil Big Run Club (LBRC) is a Melbourne-based run club. Their positioning: the run club for the everyday person — inclusive, welcoming, and unpretentious, but with a premium feel. Ethos is community-first and locally grounded. As of April 2026 they are undergoing a rebrand and commissioning a new website.

### Brand & Tone of Voice

The brand should feel:
- **Friendly and approachable** — never elitist or intimidating
- **Premium but not corporate** — clean design, quality photography, thoughtful typography
- **Community-first** — people over performance
- **Locally grounded** — unmistakably Melbourne

### Melbourne-Specific Context

- Route maps include public transport info (tram/train stops, Myki zones) and parking
- Winter runs: early darkness (sunset ~5:10pm in June); visibility gear recommended
- Post-run café partners are a genuine community draw; featured prominently
- Tied to local races: Melbourne Marathon (October), Run Melbourne, City2Sea, The Tan Ultra
- Terrain/accessibility notes per route: path surface, pram-friendly, lighting conditions
